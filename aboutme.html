About Me

I'm currently an analyst in the Chicago office of Analysis Group. I graduated from the University of Notre Dame in 2015 with a bachelor's degree in finance and economics. After graduation, I spent a year and a half in the money and payment studies group at the Federal Reserve Bank of New York.

One of my personal hobbies is following bitcoin and distributed ledger technology more generally. If you're new to bitcoin, it's definitely worth checking out some of the info at bitcoin.org. I'm currently a freelance writer for Bitcoin Magazine, and you can find articles I've written here.
